Playground Update

We are excited that we are so close to getting our new playground equipment installed. The rain has certainly slowed things down a bit, but we are getting very close now!

We will be putting one piece of equipment in each of three areas: Nursery & Toddlers, Two year olds, and Three year olds-Pre-Kindergarten. Each area will be separated by a fence so 3 classes can play outside at the same time.

We can wait to climb, slide and explore our new playground pieces!
