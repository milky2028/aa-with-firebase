Please Remember

• Keep a spare set of clothes on site (more for the younger children) for those occasional mishaps.
* Please check to make sure they are appropriate for the season.
• Make sure your child has a jacket.  The state requires us to go outside daily if not raining and over 25 degrees.
• Diapers and wipes should come in unopened packages that we can keep here.  We will let you know when we need more.
• Bottles should come in with caps and labeled with the child’s name, and the date and time the bottle was made.  The state will site us if bottles in our refrigerator are not labeled correctly.

Picture Permissions

We would love to include pictures of your children on our website.  We will soon be asking parents for permission so that we can be sure to only include pictures of children with permissions.

Parent Advisory Council

We are working towards our Paths To Quality Level 2.  One of the requirements for this level is to have a Parent Advisory Council in place.  If you would be interested in being a part please contact the ofﬁce.  Meetings can be conducted through email so we would likely only plan a face to face meeting 1-2 times per year.

Check Our New Playground!

Our new playground is finally all put together! We're so excited to have this outdoor venue where all the children of Adventure Academy can run, laugh, play, and explore in the summer sun! This is something we've been working toward for the children of our center for some time now. Outdoor play and the ability for children to exercise their large muscle groups helps improve brain development and gives children tools for strength and maturity as they grow. We've been delighted to see the faces of children thus far as they get the chance to run, jump, and climb throughout our new outdoor play center.
