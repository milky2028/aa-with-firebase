October 2017 Newsletter

Please Remember

• Keep a spare set of clothes on site (more for the younger children) for those occasional mishaps.
* Please check to make sure they are appropriate for the season.
• Make sure your child has a jacket.  The state requires us to go outside daily if not raining and over 25 degrees.
• Diapers and wipes should come in unopened packages that we can keep here.  We will let you know when we need more.
• Bottles should come in with caps and labeled with the child’s name, and the date and time the bottle was made.  The state will site us if bottles in our refrigerator are not labeled correctly.

Inclement Weather Closings

We understand that the weather in Indiana can be very unpredictable but we also understand that parents often do not have the opportunity to stay home from work when weather conditions are unfavorable.  In most instances we will NOT close for inclement weather.

We MAY close or delay our opening for the following reasons:

  • In the event we experience a major snowfall or storm and we feel it would be unsafe for our staff and families to travel.
  • In the event the city or local ofﬁcials put travel warnings in place.

If we do have a delay or closing, we will make this decision as early as possible and notify parents through the local TV channels.

When a closing must happen after the school has already opened for the day, parents will be called to come and pick up their children as quickly as possible.
