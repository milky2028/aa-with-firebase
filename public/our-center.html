Our Center

Adventure Academy Daycare Center Features:

Adventure Academy provides a number of accommodations, seeking to provide beneficial experiences not only for children, but to provide conveniences for parents as well. A few of the many features of our center are listed below:

  • Classes for children 6 weeks to 5 years
  • Adventure Academy accepts CCDF
  • Open 6:30am to 6:00pm
  • Divided large outdoor play area
  • Fresh healthy meals prepared on site and served daily
  • Full day and half day schedules available
  • Teachers with or working towards CDA
  • Minutes from Castleton Mall, Community North Hospital, and Roche Diagnostics
