Our Classes

All classes at Adventure Academy are grouped by age.

Infants

0 - 12 months

From helpless newborn to active toddler, your child will grow and learn more in their first 12 months than any other time of their life.

Learn and Play

Toddlers

12 - 24 months

Active is often the first word that comes to mind when describing a Toddler. During these 12 months, children go from walking unsteadily to running constantly.

Question and Answer

Twos

24 - 36 months

By the time a child enters his or her 2nd year, he or she has often become quite verbal and, though we may not understand every word, our attention to the stories they tell are is crucial.

Speak and Listen

Threes

36 - 48 months

Questions, questions and more questions. Three-year-olds have lots of questions. What is that? Why? Where? How? At Adventure Academy, we will seek to help your child explore the world around them while providing answers to the essential questions they ask.

Move and Explore

Fours and Fives

48 - 60 months

Four and five-year-olds are big kids, but they’re still just kids. They are learning so much so quickly and still needs lots of time to simply play. There is so much to be learned through play.

Grow and Develop

A.M. Preschool

Ages 3 - 5

AM Preschool is a great option for parents who don't need all day care, but still would like to have their children learning, playing, and interacting with other kids. This class is offered from 9:00am - 12:30am. Includes breakfast and lunch.
